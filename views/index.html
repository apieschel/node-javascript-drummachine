Drum Machine (Record, Set Tempo with MIDI, and Upload Your Own Samples)

Instructions

  • MIDI: Web browsers aren't great at keeping accurate time. If you want an accurate BPM, then you can hook up an external sampler or drum machine to your computer (via MIDI interface) to keep time. Tell the drum machine to play 16 straight sixteenth notes, and this machine in the browser will update its count every time your external drum machine plays a note. Click the "MIDI" button, and try it out! While MIDI is off, the browser will keep time on its own.
  • RECORD: Click "RECORD" to start recording your drum loop. Click the button again to stop. Then click again to turn your drum loop into an HTML audio element that you can download as a .wav file.
  • CLEAR TRACKS: Press "C" on your keyboard to clear the tracks.
  • UPLOAD: You can upload your own sample packs to play with this drum machine. Click "Choose Files." Hold down "Select" to select multiple audio files to upload from your computer. Click "Upload" once you've selected the files you want.
